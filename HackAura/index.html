About HackAura

HackAura is a national-level virtual open innovation hackathon that unites the brightest minds from across the country to innovate, collaborate, and solve real-world challenges. Our mission is to provide a vibrant platform where creativity meets technology, and raw ideas evolve into impactful, scalable solutions.

This 12-hour coding sprint invites developers, designers, and changemakers to build projects of their choice — no fixed tracks, no limits. Whether it's AI, Web3, Sustainability, or Social Good, you're free to explore any domain that excites you. Participants gain access to expert mentorship, interactive sessions, and a chance to win exciting prizes and recognition.

From ideation to execution, HackAura is more than a competition — it's a launchpad for future innovators.

Event Timeline

Jul 25 - Aug 14

Concept Submission Phase

Participants are invited to submit their innovative project proposals through our online portal. The submission platform will become accessible at the commencement of this phase.

Aug 16

Prototype Development Sprint

Selected teams will participate in an intensive 12-hour development session to transform their concepts into functional prototypes, demonstrating core functionality and technical viability.

Aug 17

Final Presentation & Judging

Teams will deliver comprehensive 5-minute presentations of their solutions, followed by a 2-minute expert Q&A session. Winners will be announced during the closing ceremony.
